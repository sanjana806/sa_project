AI Project Lifecycle

Every successful AI project follows a structured path from an idea to a functional solution. This timeline outlines the critical stages of the machine learning lifecycle.

Problem Scoping

This initial phase is about understanding the 'why'. We define the business objective and establish success metrics to ensure the AI solution aligns perfectly with the end goal.

Data Acquisition

Once the problem is defined, we identify and gather the necessary raw data. This can involve querying databases, connecting to APIs, or scraping information from the web.

Data Exploration & Preprocessing

The most time-consuming stage, where raw data is transformed into a usable format. It involves EDA, handling missing values, cleaning inconsistencies, and structuring data.

Modeling & Evaluation

Here, we select algorithms, train models, and fine-tune parameters. Each model is rigorously evaluated against metrics to determine which one performs best for the problem.

Deployment & Visualization

A successful model is one that's put to use. The model is deployed into production, and insights are communicated through interactive dashboards and visualizations.
